How to Make Tacos:

Author: Vidit Makwana

Ingrediants:
2 tortillas
1 can Goya black beans
1 cup Mexican rice
Mexican cheese
Sour Cream

Directions:
1. Warm up the tortilla on the stove
2. Add some black beans and Mexican rice on the tortilla
3. Sprinkle some Mexican cheese on top
4. Add the sour cream on top (optional)
5. Do the same for the second tortilla
